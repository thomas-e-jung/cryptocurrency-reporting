- Market  - Left value is the asset they want to buy, right value is the asset they are trading off
- Type - Refers to transaction order type:
	Limit - Buyer sets a price limit for what they want to buy.  If price doesn't hit their limit, then the transaction is not filled.
	Market - Buyer is purchasing from the first available seller, price doesn't matter.
	Ceiling - Buyer is buying any amount of asset at price. 
- Side
	- Buy - Bought the asset
	- Sell - Sold the asset
- Size - How many assets are purchased 
	- Left side is how many ordered
	- Right side is how many requested (should be equal except ceiling types)
- Price - Buyer sets price (ceiling is always 0)
- Subtotal - Before the fees
- Fees - Trading fees
